About

Hello! I'm Sebi, a senior at the University of Southern California studying Aerospace Engineering and graduating in 2024.

Since the disappearance of Malaysian Airlines Flight 370, I have been deeply passionate about aircraft safety. My current research is at USC's Aviation Safety & Security Program, helping manage the largest private collection of aviation accident materials and exploring AI applications in aircraft.

My interests also lie in trading, quantitative finance, space exploration, and sustainable aviation.

In my spare time, I'm heavily involved in Esports at USC in both the managerial and production sides. I also love to make music, whether it be self-producing my own covers or performing fingerstyle guitar melodies.
